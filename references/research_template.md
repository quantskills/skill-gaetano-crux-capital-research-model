# Research Template

Use this structure for a Gaetano-style public thesis:

1. Theme
   - Photonics, CPO, optical networking, Physical AI, robotics, simulation, sensors, or another infrastructure layer.

2. Stack Position
   - Where the company sits in the value chain.
   - Upstream materials, component, module, system, software, integration, customer, or end-market.

3. Chokepoint
   - What becomes scarce or hard to replicate.
   - Qualification cycle, capacity, physics constraint, process know-how, customer lock-in, or standards timing.

4. Evidence
   - Earnings transcript
   - Conference presentation
   - Technical paper
   - Patent or product documentation
   - Customer capex
   - Supply-chain news

5. Why Market May Miss It
   - Small cap
   - Under-covered niche
   - Hidden operating leverage
   - Technology misunderstood
   - Timing uncertainty

6. Catalyst
   - Design win, capacity ramp, standard adoption, product launch, customer qualification, margin inflection, or conference signal.

7. Risks
   - Commoditization, customer concentration, dilution, delayed qualification, competing architecture, capex cycle, liquidity, or valuation.

8. Tracking Indicators
   - Orders, backlog, gross margin, capex, product roadmap, industry standards, customer commentary, and peer read-throughs.

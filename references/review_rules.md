# Review Rules

## Keep

Keep a row when it contains a forward-looking public thesis with:

- company or technology
- stack position
- chokepoint
- evidence
- catalyst or timing
- risk or invalidation

## Deweight

Deweight when the content is:

- educational but not actionable
- a broad basket
- a watchlist
- missing timing
- missing risk
- mostly sector commentary

## Remove From Forward Signal

Remove from forward-signal analysis when it is:

- retrospective performance discussion
- portfolio recap
- marketing or credibility context
- quote-only content
- a ticker only mentioned as a comparison

## Delete

Delete rows that are paid-only, private, duplicate, irrelevant, or based on material the user does not have permission to analyze.
